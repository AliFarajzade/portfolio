Passager

Full-Stack TypeScript Tour Application.

Passager is a tour application with lots of tours and advantures!
It is built with Next, React, Recoil (State manager), TailwindCSS and Sass on the front-end with server-side rendering, and Node, Express and MongoDB on the back-end side and It's hosted on Heroku and Vercel. It includes features like authentication with JWT, complex queries, cool animations and maps!
